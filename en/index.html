Getting Started Guide

1

Download the app

Install MyDay from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad. The app requires iOS 17.0 or later.

2

Allow Calendar and Reminders

On first launch, the app requests access to your Calendars and Reminders. Before each permission, an explanation is shown to tell you why that access is needed. iOS does not offer read-only access to Calendar, but MyDay will never create or modify any event or reminder.

3

Choose your calendars and reminders

In Settings, select exactly which calendars and reminder lists you want displayed on your home screen.

4

Enable optional features

In Settings, you can activate at your own pace: Photos (with album selection), Activity data (steps, distance, calories), Quote of the Day, and Daily Horoscope. Each activation is preceded by a clear explanation.

5

Create custom links

In Settings ‚Üí Custom Links, associate Shortcuts with your recurring events or reminders. A tap on the item in your agenda launches the action directly.

6

Enable iCloud (optional)

If you use multiple Apple devices, make sure iCloud is enabled to automatically sync your preferences and custom links.

Custom Links Guide

Custom links allow you to associate Shortcuts with your events and reminders. A simple tap on your agenda instantly triggers the desired action.

1

Create a shortcut in the Shortcuts app

Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone and create a new shortcut. For example:

  • Open Zoom: "Open URLs" action with your meeting link
  • Call: "Receive Text" action then "Call [Input Text]"
  • Navigation: "Get Directions to [Address]" action
  • Music: "Play Playlist [Name]" action

üí° Tip: To receive parameters, add the "Receive Text" action at the beginning of your shortcut.

2

Configure the link in MyDay

In MyDay, go to Settings ‚Üí Custom Links:

  1. Tap the + button
  2. Enter the Keyword that appears in your events (e.g., "Zoom", "Call", "Gym")
  3. Choose your Shortcut from the list
  4. Save

üí° The keyword must match exactly the beginning of your event titles (case insensitive).

3

Use the link in your agenda

Create an event or reminder whose title starts with your keyword. A üîó icon will automatically appear next to the item.

Examples:

  • "Zoom team meeting" ‚Üí Keyword: "Zoom" ‚Üí Opens your Zoom link
  • "Gym cardio session" ‚Üí Keyword: "Gym" ‚Üí Launches your workout playlist
  • "Reminder take medication" ‚Üí Keyword: "Reminder" ‚Üí Opens Health app
4

Pass parameters to your shortcut

Use the format Keyword : parameter in your event title to pass information to the shortcut.

Advanced examples:

  • "Call : Marie" ‚Üí The shortcut receives "Marie" as input
  • "Navigate : 123 Example Street" ‚Üí The shortcut receives the address
  • "Email : Monthly Report" ‚Üí The shortcut receives the subject
  • "Message : Hello" ‚Üí The shortcut receives the message text

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Your shortcut must contain the "Receive Text" action to use the parameter.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

MyDay requires iOS 17.0 or later. The app is compatible with iPhone and iPad.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your devices and in your personal iCloud. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

Is the app free?

MyDay costs $1 USD but is entirely ad-free with no in-app purchases. All features are included.

Can I use the app without iCloud?

Yes, the app works perfectly without iCloud. You will only lose synchronization of your preferences and custom links between devices.

Why does the app request access to my Calendar and Reminders?

These two permissions are necessary for the app to function. A detailed explanation is shown before each request. Regarding Calendar, iOS does not offer read-only access ‚Äî MyDay will never create, modify, or delete any event.

Why aren't Photos and Health requested at launch?

These features are entirely optional. You enable them yourself in Settings, only if you want to. A clear explanation is shown before each permission so you can make an informed decision.

Is the quote of the day translated?

Yes. If your device is set to a language other than English, the quote is automatically translated before being displayed. No English version will appear.

How do I change my zodiac sign or horoscope source?

In Settings ‚Üí Horoscope configuration, you can change your sign and choose from the available sources. The source in use is indicated below each horoscope displayed.

What are custom links?

Custom links let you associate a Shortcuts action with an event or reminder in your agenda. For example, a tap on "Zoom Meeting" can directly open your Zoom link. A üîó icon appears next to items that have an associated link.

Can I pass information to a Shortcut?

Yes. If you use the format Keyword: parameter in your event or reminder title, everything after the colon is automatically passed to the Shortcut. For example, "Call: Marie" will launch your "Phone" shortcut and pass "Marie" as the text input. The Shortcut must contain a "Receive text" action to use that data.

What does the üë• icon next to an event mean?

This icon indicates that the event or reminder comes from a shared calendar or list (e.g. "Family", "Team", "Couple"). It lets you quickly distinguish your personal commitments from those shared with other people.

How do I return to MyDay after running a shortcut?

Use the URL MyDay:// to return directly to the app. Simply add an "Open URLs" action with MyDay:// at the end of your Shortcuts workflow. After the shortcut finishes, you will be automatically redirected back to MyDay.

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

At MyDay, your privacy is our top priority. We designed our app with privacy by design principles. Every permission is explained before being requested.

Data Collection

We collect NO personal data. The app only accesses information you choose to share (Calendars, Reminders, and optionally HealthKit and Photos) and this data remains exclusively on your devices.

Permissions Requested

On first launch: Calendar and Reminders ‚Äî necessary to display your agenda. An explanation is provided before each request.
On demand (in Settings): Photos and Health data ‚Äî only if you decide to enable them. None of these permissions are required to use the app.

What the app does not do

MyDay never creates, modifies, or deletes any event or reminder. It never modifies any Health data. It never uploads or transfers your photos to our servers (we have none).

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable it, synchronized through your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your information.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Ever.

Third-Party Services

MyDay does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services. The app contains no trackers. The quote of the day and horoscope sources are read-only third-party APIs ‚Äî no personal data is sent to them.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can revoke access permissions at any time in iOS settings. Deleting the app erases all local data.
