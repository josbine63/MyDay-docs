Getting Started Guide

1

Download the app

Install MyDay from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad. The app requires iOS 17.0 or later.

2

Grant permissions

On first launch, grant permissions for Activity (Steps, Distance, and Calories), Photos, Calendars, and Reminders. These permissions are necessary to fully enjoy the app.

3

Enable iCloud

Make sure iCloud is enabled on your devices to benefit from automatic data synchronization.

4

Customize your experience

Explore the app settings to customize the display and notifications according to your preferences.

5

Choose your calendars

Select which calendars you want to display in the app to keep track of your important events.

6

Choose your reminder lists

Select which reminder lists you want to see to stay on top of your tasks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

MyDay requires iOS 17.0 or later. The app is compatible with iPhone and iPad.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your devices and in your personal iCloud. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

Is the app free?

No, MyDay costs $1 USD but is ad-free with no in-app purchases. Enjoy all features without hidden fees.

Can I use the app without iCloud?

Yes, the app works without iCloud, but you'll lose automatic synchronization between your devices. Your data will remain stored locally on each device.

Why does the app request access to my data?

MyDay requests access to display your Activity data, Photos, Calendars, and Reminders to organize your day. These permissions are necessary for the app to function properly, but your data always remains private.

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

At MyDay, your privacy is our top priority. We designed our app with privacy by design principles.

Data Collection

We collect NO personal data. The app only accesses information you choose to share (HealthKit, Photos, Calendars, Reminders) and this data remains exclusively on your devices.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable it, synchronized through your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your information.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Ever.

Third-Party Services

MyDay does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services. The app contains no trackers.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can revoke access permissions at any time in iOS settings. Deleting the app erases all local data.

Policy Changes

Any changes to this policy will be communicated through an app update and published on this page.
